Special Events & Tours

Guided Tours

We offer guided tours of our exhibitions every Saturday at 2:00 PM. Tours are free with gallery admission and last approximately 45 minutes.

Private Tours

For groups of 10 or more, we can arrange private tours outside of regular hours. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Artist Talks

Join us for monthly artist talks where featured artists discuss their work and creative process. Check our events calendar for upcoming talks.

Educational Programs

We offer workshops and educational programs for schools and community groups. Contact our education coordinator for more information.

Accessibility

Gallery Accessibility Features

  • Wheelchair Accessible: All areas of the gallery are wheelchair accessible
  • Audio Tours: Available for visually impaired visitors
  • Sign Language: Interpreters available with 48-hour notice
  • Service Animals: Welcome in all areas of the gallery
  • Large Print: Exhibition materials available in large print
  • Assistive Technology: Available for visitors with disabilities
  • Accessible Parking: Designated spaces available
  • Accessible Restrooms: Located on the main floor

Location & Directions

How to Find Us

From Downtown:
Take Main Street north for 3 blocks, turn right onto Art Street. We're on the left side, 2 blocks down.

From Highway:
Exit 15, follow Creative District signs. Turn left onto Art Street at the third traffic light.

Public Transportation:
• Bus: Routes 15, 22, 45 stop directly in front of the gallery
• Subway: Creative District Station (Line A), 2-minute walk
• Bike: Bike racks available in front of the gallery

Parking:
• Street parking: Available on Art Street and surrounding blocks
• Parking garage: 2 blocks away on Main Street
• Accessible parking: Available in front of the gallery
